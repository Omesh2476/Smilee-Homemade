Our Story

Made with heart,
eaten with joy.

Smilee Homemade was born out of a simple belief: food tastes best when it is made at home, with fresh ingredients and without any shortcuts. We started making jams and spreads for our family, and the smiles they brought made us want to share them with everyone.

Every batch we make is small, seasonal, and crafted by hand — because that is the only way we know how to do it right. No artificial preservatives, no palm oil, no additives. Just honest, real flavours that bring homemade happiness to your table.
